About

Kingdom of Valand is a social / party game where players are challenged to deduce one another's identities while completing quests.

In order to play, you need at least 4 players in an environment where players can communicate to each others. Although you can play with 4 players, It is recommended to have at least 5 players for better experience - the more the merrier!

There are 2 important rules that each players must follow to enjoy this game:

  • DO NOT show your screen to others
  • DO NOT see other player's screen

    • Players are allowed to start conversation at any point in the game. Discussion, claims, accusation, deception, bluffs, and logical deduction are important as a strategy to win the game.

    Rules

    Game Objective

    There are 2 opposing teams within the game:

    Kingdom of Valand

    As a Loyal servants of Valand's, your team will win if 3 out of 5 quests are successfully completed.

    Erador Clan

    As the Eradors, your team will win if 3 out of 5 quests ends in failure.
    Erador Clan can also win by assassinating Eldridge at the end of the game (when both Eldridge and Asellus are used during the play).

    Identity

    Inside the game you will be assigned a random character. Make sure to check your character at the start of the game so that you know which side you are on, and the special abilities you can do.

    To check your character click on the "SPECIAL" button located on the bottom right corner of your screen

    Please Note: in a 4 players game, Eldridge and Hugo will not be available.

    Game Play

    The game are composed of 5 rounds. Each rounds is divided into 3 phases:

    Team Assignment Phase

    The leader proposes a team to complete a quest. Each player will take turn to be the leader through out the rounds.

    Voting Phase

    Once team has been selected by the leader, all players will have to vote either to approve the proposed team or reject the proposed team. To get approved, the majority should approve the proposed team; a draw is considered as rejection. When approved, the proposed team will go to the quest phase, otherwise the leadership is passed on to the next player and repeating the process until a team is approved.

    When 5 consecutive rejections are made, the Erador Clan will win the game automatically

    Eldridge may use voting phase to help Valands people, but becareful not to become too obvious as Asellus might be able to assasinate at the end of the game

    Quest Phase

    Those players selected in the approved team will determine if the Quest is successfull. One failure submission is enough to failed the Quest except on the 4th Quest (and only the 4th quest) in games of 7 or more players, it will require at least 2 failures quest submission to be considered as a failed Quest.

    After each quest completion, voting track is reset.

    Becareful, Erador's player may submit a successful quest as a disguise

    End Game

    The game will end immediately after 3 successful or 3 failed quests; or when 5 consecutive rejections occurs.

    In the event where Kingdom of Valand wins the game and Asellus is in the game, there will be a last chance for the Erador Clan. The game will enter in Assassination Phase

    Assassination Phase

    Asellus may take this opportunity to discuss with his team mates to guess who is Eldridge. If Asellus guesses right, Erador Clan wins the game.
